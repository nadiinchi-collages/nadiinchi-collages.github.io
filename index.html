Collages by Nadia Chirkova

Hi! I am Nadia, a Deep Learning Research Scientist.
My hobby is making collages, and I created this website to share my works with those who may be interested in them.
My collages are usually inspired by time spent with my family and friends, trips or walks.
I am not a professional artist, so don't be strict! :)
